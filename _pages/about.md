About me
========

Hi! Welcome to my personal webpage. I am an aspiring theoretical particle physicist from India. I did my BS and MS from the Indian Institute of Science Education and Research, Kolkata. I did my MS thesis at IIT Guwahati under the supervision of Prof. Subhaditya Bhattacharya on Lepton Number Violation in the Standard Model Effective Field Theory.
